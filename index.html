BRIEF HISTORY

During the time of one of the major covid lockdown of 2020, the Scripture Union students, the PNG Bible church,the Luthren and the Inter-denominational students at the Sogeri National school of excellence came together to conduct their end of year function. It was a mini function organised for the out going executives of the relegious student's leaders of that year. Although it was a tough year God was doing so much for us, this include the amazing idea to create a group that can unite us as one body of christs after knowing what was waiting for us back at home; the relegious, cultural and other physical barriers will distanced us after leaving school.
The idea about this move was brought to life one year after the actual discussion however a few students make an attenpt to conduct mini crusades in their home provinces. Since most of the members were selected to the University of Papua New Guinea,a commitee was then formed. Since then not only do we have members from Sogeri but students from across PNG as well as graduants and proffessionals and other likeminded people from local churches in the country.
After all it not only help others but the members as well in physicsl and mentall aspect as well.
